# Security and Responsible Use

## Scope

This repository is a public Codex skill for lawful PRC legal-document workflows, structured review, evidence organization, and source-based compliance support.

It is not intended for:

- fake law, fake citations, fake judicial materials, or fake evidence
- forgery, false litigation, false stamping, or tampering workflows
- debt evasion, asset concealment, regulatory evasion, or coercive abuse
- unauthorized collection or misuse of protected data
- public release of confidential client files or matter files

## Reporting

If you discover:

- a script behavior that materially conflicts with the documented lawful-use boundaries
- a bundled reference that creates avoidable legal, licensing, or confidentiality risk
- a packaging mistake such as private client content, sensitive screenshots, or proprietary source material

open an issue in the repository or remove the repository from public distribution until it is corrected.

## Maintenance expectations

- Keep the skill aligned with current documented legal-source practices.
- Do not merge client files, real confidential case materials, or proprietary database exports into this repo.
- Keep refusal boundaries and anti-abuse notes aligned with actual skill behavior.
- Re-check public-source references when legal baselines materially change.
- Treat all source-monitoring, OCR, PDF, and calculation output as working support, not as a guarantee of filing success or legal outcome.

## No warranty

This repository includes structured workflows and helper scripts, not guarantees.

Legal correctness still depends on current law, verified facts, source integrity, jurisdiction-specific rules, and human sign-off in the relevant matter context.
